Tanzania: Law on Child Rights Enacted in Zanzibar

On August 25, 2011, Dr. Ali Mohamed Shein, the President of Zanzibar, a semi-autonomous island region in the United Republic of Tanzania, signed into law a comprehensive children's rights law. (Shane Keenan, Children's Act Provides New Tools for Protecting Child Rights in Zamzibar [sic], UNICEF (Aug. 25, 2011).)
